Avocados? Why?

Avocados are green, pear-shaped fruit loaded with important nutrients. A single serving of avocado contains over 10% of your recommended daily allowance of Vitamins B5, B6, C, E as well as over 20% of your recommended daily allowance of Folate, and Vitmain K. Thats in addition to the Magnesium, Manganese, Copper, Iron, Zinc, Phosphorous, Vitamin A, B1, B2, and B3 that they contain. Wow!

Don't forget Potassium, most Americans do. An average adult only gets half of their recommended daily allowance of Potassium. [Source] Avocados are loaded with potassium, containing 140% more than a banana. You don't miss out on any important nutrients when you eat avocados, especially fresh ones from Avocado Emporium. Delivered to your door just days after they're picked off the tree and ensured for the utmost quality, our avocados contain more nutrients than any plain old avocado from a grocery store.

Company History

Avocado Emporium was founded on a simple dream all the way back in 1965, to make avocados great again. From a lowly farmhouse in southern California to a world leader in agriculture, Avocado Emporium has strived to reach that goal through innovation and determination for over 50 years. Avocado Emporium approved farmers use only the best methods and techniques to ensure that our avocados are the freshest and best tasting avocados on this planet. We are the leading employer of avocado farmers in the world, ensuring fair working conditions and wages for all of our employees, as well as the best health care in the agriculture industry.
